ARKs

R74n has been assigned NAAN 49595, making it possible for us to mint Archival Resource Keys, or ARKs.

R74n ARKs are defined under ark:/49595/.

Resources in our ARKs are prefixed with a standard

Main NodePurposeExample
ark:/49595/mpMultiplane, in Hexadecimal Planecode formatark:/49595/mpR001F8
ark:/49595/wbR74n Wikibase, in QID, PID, or LID formatark:/49595/wbQ123
ark:/49595/wbP123
ark:/49595/wbL44
ark:/49595/amMulti-Letter Alpha Namespaceark:/49595/amsandboxels
ark:/49595/atTwo-Letter Alpha Namespaceark:/49595/atsb
ark:/49595/rqRequestedark:/49595/rq123

ARKs can be resolved with our ID Resolver or through N2T.net, like so:

https://R74n.com/id?ark:/49595/mpR00111

https://n2t.net/ark:49595/mpR00111
