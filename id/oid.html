Object Identifiers

An Object Identifier (OID) is a unique number that identifies anything!

It looks like this: 1.3.6.1.4.1.61117

The owner of 1.3.6 can assign numbers to its children to mean anything. 1.3.6.1 is the ID for the Internet!

1.3.6.1.4.1.61117 is the OID for R74n. You can learn more in our guidelines document.

We are giving out OIDs per request! Yours would look like this: 1.3.6.1.4.1.61117.9000.123

  1. Contact us, either through email (contact@R74n.com) or another way.
  2. Once we reply with your OID, create a document somewhere containing your children IDs and any other info, like ours.

That's it? Yes!!

You can identify your projects, yourself, your friends.. really anything you'd like!
