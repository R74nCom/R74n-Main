AIDs

R74n controls an AID, or Application Identifier (ISO/IEC 7816), granted under ViaThinkSoft's FreeOID system.

The root AID is D2 76 00 01 86 F1 17 6F, with our part in bold.

Identifiers under D2 76 00 01 86 F1 17 6F FF 00 [PIX], where [PIX] is a lowercase Two-letter Alpha Code in hexadecimal, represent R74n projects

For example, Sandboxels is represented by D2 76 00 01 86 F1 17 6F FF 00 73 62, for "sb".

More AIDs can be found in our AID Repository.
