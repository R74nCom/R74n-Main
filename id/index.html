R74n uses many standards to identify projects and concepts, some proprietary and others commonplace.

Use the form above to redirect to pages identified by an ID. (e.g. R74n.com/id?urn:sandboxels:changelog). It supports R74n URNs, Alpha Namespaces, UUIDs, QIDs, URLs, Multiplane hex & decimal planecodes, and some OIDs in dot and ASN.1 notation.
