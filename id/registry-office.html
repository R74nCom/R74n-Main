OID

Object Identifier (OID): 1.3.6.1.4.1.61117.9000.xxxxx

Your OID supports the creation of children in an infinite tree-like fashion. For example, one of your child OIDs can be 1.3.6.1.4.1.61117.9000.xxxxx.123. Please keep a document or list somewhere online with all of your children's names, similar to our catalog. All that is really needed is the OID and name, everything else is optional, anything you define is automatically valid forever.
