Quality of Life


Quality of Life is a huge datapack for vanilla Minecraft with other 900 recipes and many more gameplay improvements.

Click here to download for 1.19!

Simply place the ZIP into the datapacks folder in your save directory to install.

Features

Crafting:
  • Over 900 total crafting recipes
  • Recipe unlocking support - recipes will appear in the recipe book
  • Universal Crafting - craft things with alternate items, like a stone pickaxe using bamboo and andesite!
  • Dyed items can be re-dyed
  • Craftable Vines & Lily Pads
  • More block uncrafting, like Quartz Blocks, Magma Blocks, Wool into String, and Mushroom Blocks
  • Craftable Name Tags, Horse Armor, Cobwebs, Ores
  • Fixable Anvils
  • Diagonal and more shapeless crafting recipes
  • Recipes for desert and Nether survival
Smelting:
  • Smelt most iron and gold items into nuggets, in the blast furnace as well
  • Smelt slabs, stairs, and walls into their smooth variants
  • Cook tropical fish
  • Burn most wooden and organic items in the blast furnace
Stonecutting:
  • Cut stairs into slabs
  • Smooth stairs and slabs
  • Cut wood into slabs, stairs, fences, and signs
  • Grind meats and other items into dye
Gameplay:
  • End Rods and Iron Bars can be climbed
  • Silk Touch can save the state of some items, like Lamps, Daylight Detectors, Bells, Campfires, and more!
  • Bamboo can be placed on more blocks
  • Banners and Carpets can be mined faster with Shears
  • Items made from valuables can be used as Beacon activators
  • Beacons as Beacon bases?!
  • Stronghold libraries can hold Knowledge Books
Commands:
  • /function give:<item> - Quick presets for items with NBT
  • /function f:clear_lag - Kills all projectiles, falling blocks, fireworks, effect clouds, and TNT
  • /function f:summon - A few quick presets for falling blocks with NBT
  • /function heads:<head> - Many give commands for custom heads
  • /function rp:<item> - Gives Education Edition chemistry items recreated on Java
