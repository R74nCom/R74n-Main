Mass Bans of January 2021

January 6, 2021
  • T supporters directed towards the Capitol in D.C., quickly break out into violence, storm the building
  • Pipe b*mbs found at the RNC and DNC
  • The first death reported
  • T posts video to multiple platforms urging peace, but also that the election was stolen and that the rioters are special and he loves them
  • Twitter locks T's account for 12 hours, removes and flags video and multiple tweets
  • Facebook and YouTube remove video
  • Facebook and Instagram accounts locked for 24 hours
  • Snapchat locks T's account indefinitely
  • Twitch removes PogChamp emote after Gootecks comments on riot
January 7, 2021
  • Twitch locks T's account indefinitely
  • Facebook and Instagram accounts locked until at least after January 20
  • TikTok bans video of T inciting riot, blocks related hashtags
  • YouTube strengthens enforcement on voter fraud claims, handing out strikes to offenders
  • False satire article of MySpace and Friendster banning T spreads
  • Twitter permanently bans L. Lin Wood and the "Q Shaman", and Dan Bongino temporarily
  • PayPal removes T-affiliated accounts
  • Shopify removes T-affiliated accounts
January 8, 2021
  • Twitter permanently bans @realDonaldTrump
  • Twitter permanently bans Sci-Hub, Sidney Powell, Michael Flynn, Ben Garrison, and Ron Watkins
  • @POTUS account posts on @realDonaldTrump's behalf, tweets gets deleted minutes later, account locked
  • In the @POTUS tweet, T alludes to creating a new social media platform
  • @TeamTrump account posts on @realDonaldTrump's behalf, gets banned minutes later
  • Gary Coby tweets on @realDonaldTrump's behalf, gets swiftly banned
  • @TrumpWarRoom continues to tweet, not on @realDonaldTrump's behalf
  • False rumors of T joining Parler spread
  • Apple warns Parler of removal from App Store in 24 hours, backfiring and making it the #1 free app
  • Google removes Parler from Google Play
  • Discord bans server for the banned subreddit r/The_Donald
  • Reddit bans r/DonaldTrump
  • Pinterest removes items alluding to overriding the election
January 9, 2021
  • Apple removes Parler from App Store
  • Amazon warns Parler they will be booting them off of AWS on the 10th at 11:59pm PST (1:59am EST)
  • Twilio stops services with Parler, removing phone and email verification
  • Wave of spam accounts take over Parler, not needing a real email or phone number
  • Parler disables registration
January 10, 2021
  • Stripe stops processing payments for T's campaign
  • Okta blocks Parler's access to free trial version of their product
January 11, 2021
  • Parler taken off AWS by Amazon, shutting the website down
  • Zendesk stops services with Parler at same time, removing customer support
  • Parler files lawsuit against Amazon
January 13, 2021
  • YouTube issues a strike to T's channel, preventing video uploads for at least 7 days
January 14, 2021
  • Snapchat permanently bans T's account
January 19, 2021
  • YouTube extends T's ban for another week
January 20, 2021
  • Biden is inaugarated
  • T and others banned from entering China
  • Twitch permanently bans T
