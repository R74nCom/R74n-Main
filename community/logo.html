Logo Project

We are accepting submissions for our Community Logo Project.

The concept is simple: Create a version of the R74n logo in a creative format. Either...

  • Draw your own version of the R74n logo, either digitally or physically.
  • Recreate the R74n logo in an interesting way. For example: in graphing software, in a video game, in-real-life, etc.

You may submit your creation(s) in the dedicated channel of our Discord server. If you can’t do that, feel free to send it to our email (contact@R74n.com), or any other way.

All logo submissions will be displayed on a gallery page!

Please ensure your submissions are square-shaped (1:1 ratio), and let us know your preferred name (or if you'd like to be anonymous), otherwise the first one we see will be publicly posted. Can’t wait to see your art!
